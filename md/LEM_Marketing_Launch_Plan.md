# LEM English Website - Marketing & Launch Plan

## Overview
This document outlines the comprehensive marketing strategy and launch plan for the LEM English Website, designed to maximize visibility for "LEM代理" keywords while establishing LiTong Electronics as the premier destination for LEM sensor solutions globally. The plan focuses on SEO dominance, content marketing, and strategic outreach to target audiences.

## Marketing Goals

### Primary Objectives
1. **SEO Leadership**: Achieve top 3 rankings for "LEM代理" and "LEM代理商" keywords on Baidu within 6 months
2. **Traffic Growth**: Generate 50,000 monthly organic visitors within 12 months
3. **Lead Generation**: Capture 500+ qualified leads per month through contact forms and sample requests
4. **Brand Authority**: Establish LiTong as the go-to "LEM代理商" in the Chinese market
5. **International Reach**: Develop 20% of traffic from international markets within 12 months

### Secondary Objectives
1. **Content Authority**: Publish 100+ technical articles establishing E-E-A-T
2. **Social Proof**: Collect 100+ customer testimonials and case studies
3. **Market Share**: Increase LEM sensor market share in target segments by 15%
4. **Knowledge Base**: Create comprehensive resource library for engineers
5. **Regional Expansion**: Establish presence in at least 5 international markets

## Pre-Launch Activities

### 1. Content Preparation Phase (Weeks 1-3)
- **Product Content Creation**: Complete all product category pages with 200-300 word descriptions
- **Technical Article Development**: Create 20 foundational technical articles
- **Solution Pages**: Develop 5 industry-specific solution pages
- **News Content**: Prepare 10 initial news articles covering company updates and industry trends
- **FAQ Compilation**: Create 50+ product and service related FAQs

### 2. Technical Preparation (Weeks 2-4)
- **SEO Setup**: Implement all technical SEO elements and structured data
- **Analytics Configuration**: Set up Google Analytics 4, Search Console, and performance tracking
- **Security Implementation**: Complete all security measures and SSL setup
- **Performance Optimization**: Optimize for Core Web Vitals targets
- **Testing Phase**: Complete cross-browser and device testing

### 3. Stakeholder Preparation (Weeks 3-4)
- **Team Training**: Train FAE team on new website features and content
- **Sales Alignment**: Prepare sales team for increased inquiry volume
- **Support Setup**: Configure CRM and inquiry management systems
- **Content Approval**: Review all content for technical accuracy
- **Legal Review**: Verify compliance with distributor agreements
